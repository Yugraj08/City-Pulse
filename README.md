# 🌆 CityPulse AI

### AI-Powered Urban Infrastructure Digital Twin for Predictive Smart City Management

CityPulse AI is an intelligent smart city platform that helps municipal authorities detect, analyze, predict, and prioritize urban infrastructure issues using Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, GIS, and Predictive Analytics.

## 📖 Overview

CityPulse AI transforms traditional complaint-based infrastructure management into a predictive AI-driven system.

Instead of waiting for citizens to report potholes or waterlogging, the platform analyzes uploaded images, complaint text, historical records, weather data, and GIS information to predict infrastructure failures before they become critical.

The system acts as a Digital Twin of the city's infrastructure, helping authorities make smarter maintenance decisions.
